About Dairy Flat Airways

New Zealand's premier boutique airline offering unique regional routes

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Dairy Flat Airways was born from a vision to connect New Zealand's most beautiful destinations with comfortable, efficient air travel. Based at Dairy Flat Airport just north of Auckland, we operate a fleet of modern light jets providing exclusive service to select destinations.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond just getting you from point A to point B. We strive to make each journey memorable, comfortable, and uniquely Kiwi.

Our Fleet

We take pride in our carefully selected fleet of modern aircraft, chosen for their efficiency, comfort, and environmental performance.

Safety First

At Dairy Flat Airways, safety is our absolute priority. Our pilots undergo rigorous training programs, and our aircraft are maintained to the highest standards, exceeding regulatory requirements.

We conduct comprehensive safety checks before every flight and continuously monitor weather conditions to ensure the safest possible journey for our passengers.
